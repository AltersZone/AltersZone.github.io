Why Alters?

With missions to help users develop healthy study, social and daily behaviors, we design every interaction to help people develop good habits on independent thinking, deep effective sharing and memorizing important events. We believe a better social relationship without privacy intruding and messaging manipulations. We help people build confidence in managing their daily life, study, work and social interactions with tools and designs that have scientifically backed theories.

Supported by the world-class psychologies and behavior scientists from UCLA, the Alters team started the development in 2021. We are currently testing the beta version at the UCLA campus, and rapidly improve and update the application with real-time feedbacks.
